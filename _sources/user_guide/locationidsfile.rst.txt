LOCATION_IDS_FILE
=================

CSV file that contains the arrival location (i.e., node) of each patient if historical data is used (``LOAD_INPUT_DATA=True``). The table contains an example. In the example, patients 0, 1 and 2 are located at nodes 18, 4 and 5 when their call arrives, respectively.

.. list-table:: Example LOCATION_IDS_FILE.
   :widths: 5
   :header-rows: 1

   * - 0
   * - 18
   * - 4
   * - 5
